Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of our most frequently asked questions by customers.
If you do not find the answer to your question below please contact us.
Why do I need a real estate agent?
Real estate agents offer a wide range of beneficial services and are very well versed in the real estate industry. This includes expertise on best marketing strategies, negotiation techniques, and how to use connections.
How long will it take to sell my property?
This depends upon a variety of different factors like house condition, asking price, etc. The best course of action would be to speak with us directly.
What are “closing costs”?
Closing costs are fees associated with the purchase of a house. Some must be paid by the seller and some by the buyer. Some examples include appraisal, inspection, and title fees. Speak with us for a more detailed explanation!
How much commission do you charge?
Granite Real Estate will charge a commission of 5%-6% upon the completion of the sale.
Do I need a home inspection?
Yes, a house inspection is necessary to identify issues and/or dangers that may exist within the home.
Do I need a down payment to buy a house?
In most cases, a down payment of 10%-20% is required, but that is on a case-by-case basis.
How do I know what I can spend on a home purchase?
Be honest with what you can afford. There are preapproval services that can help you identify a threshold.
How do I know how much to list my house for?
By speaking with our agents you will get a look at the competitve market and identify the best price to attract buyers.
What is the selling/buying process like?
Buying and selling a house have similar processes. Preparing, gathering documents, working out finances, and other steps. Speak with us to get a detailed overview of the entire process.
How much experience does Granite Real Estate have in real estate?
Granite Real Estate has been providing exceptional real estate services for the last 15 years. We are very well equipped to handle any situation.
