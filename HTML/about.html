Who Is Granite Real Estate?

Granite Real Estate is a small real estate agency owned by broker Brady Hill and located in Peru, Vermont, the town known for its small population and for its contribution to the movie Baby Boom. We have been providing service to our clients for 15 years.
Fun fact: The company started in the owner's home. After growing through word-of-mouth and repeat business, we moved the company to its present location.

We care about our clients. That's why we want to assist you. We sell and rent great homes at affordable prices, and we only offer you the best deal. We believe good homes contribute to a good life and it's our responsibility to get you prepared. If your're thinking of purchasing we can help you decide.
We don't help you buy your own home, we help you buy your dream.
