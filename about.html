We are Granite Real Estate

Professional. Friendly. Productive.

Granite Real Estate is a small real estate agency owned by broker Brady Hill and located in Peru, Vermont. You know, the town known for its small population and for its contribution to the movie Baby Boom.

We care about our clients. That's why we want to assist you. We sell and rent great homes at affordable prices, and we only offer you the best deal. We believe good homes contribute to a good life and it's our responsibility to get you prepared. If your're thinking of purchasing we can help you decide.
We don't help you buy your own home, we help you buy your dream.
